How to Use Ralph's Remove BG

  • 🧾 How to Use Ralph's Remove BG
  • Upload Your Image
  • Click the "Choose image" button or drag and drop your image into the upload box.
  • Supported formats: PNG, JPEG. For best results, use images with clear backgrounds.
  • Choose Removal Mode
  • Auto: Samples corners to detect background color.
  • Eyedropper: Click on the image to select the background color manually.
  • Pick Color: Use the color picker to choose a background color.
  • Adjust Settings
  • Tolerance: Controls how much color variation is removed. Higher = more aggressive.
  • Feather: Softens the edges for smoother cutouts.
  • Invert: Flips the selection if needed.
  • Remove Background
  • Click "Remove background" to process the image.
  • Preview the result on the right canvas.
  • Download Your Image
  • Once satisfied, click "Download PNG" to save your image with a transparent background.
  • You can add this guide directly to your site or link to it from a "Help" or "How it Works" section.

    • 🛠 Common Issues and Fixes

  • No image preview or result?
  • Make sure you uploaded a valid image file (PNG or JPEG).
  • Check that the image isn't too large (ideally under 12MB).
  • Try clicking "Remove background" after uploading.
  • Blank canvas or no transparency?
  • You may need to adjust the tolerance slider to better match your image's background color.
  • Try switching to Eyedropper mode and click directly on the background in the image preview.
  • Download button disabled?
  • The "Download PNG" button only activates after background removal is processed.
  • If it's still disabled, try clicking "Remove background" again.
  • JavaScript errors or nothing happens?
  • Make sure you're using a modern browser like Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.
  • Open your browser's developer console (F12) and check for any error messages.
